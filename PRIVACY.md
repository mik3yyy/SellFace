# Privacy Policy

**SellFace**
Last updated: 28 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SellFace ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the SellFace app.

---

## What We Collect

### Photos You Upload
When you create a person in SellFace, you upload photos of yourself. These photos are transmitted to our AI processing partner (Astria AI) to train a personalised AI model and generate your headshots. Your photos are used solely for this purpose.

### Generated Images
The AI-generated headshots created from your photos are stored securely on Cloudinary, our cloud storage provider, so you can access and download them within the app.

### Device Identifier
We generate an anonymous device identifier when you first open the app. This identifier is stored on your device and on our servers and is used to associate your personas and generated images with your account. It is not linked to your name, email address, or Apple ID.

### iCloud Record ID (optional)
If iCloud is available on your device, we may store an anonymised iCloud record identifier alongside your device identifier. This helps us restore your account if you reinstall the app. We do not access any iCloud data beyond this identifier.

### Push Notification Token
If you grant notification permission, we collect your device push token to notify you when your AI headshots are ready. This token is stored on our servers and is not shared with advertising networks.

### Purchase Information
In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details. We receive only confirmation that a purchase was completed in order to unlock the relevant style bundle.

---

## How We Use Your Data

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Uploaded photos | AI model training and headshot generation |
| Generated images | Storage and delivery within the app |
| Device identifier | Account management and data retrieval |
| iCloud record ID | Account restoration after reinstall |
| Push token | Delivery notifications for completed headshots |
| Purchase confirmation | Unlocking purchased style bundles |

We do not use your data for advertising, behavioural profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the SellFace service.

---

## Third Parties

### Astria AI
Your uploaded photos and generation requests are sent to Astria AI for processing. Astria AI acts as a data processor on our behalf. You can review their privacy practices at [astria.ai](https://www.astria.ai).

### Cloudinary
Generated images and uploaded photos are stored on Cloudinary's secure cloud infrastructure. You can review their privacy practices at [cloudinary.com/privacy](https://cloudinary.com/privacy).

### Apple
In-app purchases are handled by Apple via StoreKit. Push notifications are delivered via Apple Push Notification service (APNs). iCloud record identifiers are read via CloudKit. Apple's Privacy Policy applies to these interactions: [apple.com/privacy](https://www.apple.com/privacy).

### Render
Our backend server is hosted on Render. Your data transits through and is stored on Render's infrastructure. You can review their privacy practices at [render.com/privacy](https://render.com/privacy).

We do not sell your data to any third party.

---

## Data Retention

- **Uploaded photos and AI models**: Retained for as long as your persona exists in the app. Deleting a persona removes the associated data from our servers.
- **Generated images**: Retained until you delete the associated persona or request deletion.
- **Device identifier and push token**: Retained until you request deletion or uninstall the app.
